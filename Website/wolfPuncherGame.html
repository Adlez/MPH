WolfPuncher currently only works in the Google Chrome browser.
This game was made as a solo project in 4 and a half weeks while learning javascript.

The Mayor is a large woman, she has been given the task of defeating all of the
wolves that are appearing all over the island. The bishop has given her this task due to
her previous experience in slaying wolves.
Her stamina is endless, her fists stronger then steel. The Mayor is an unstoppable
monster, but if she gets bored or distracted he quest will end. Running into rocks
will annoy her, and killing bunnies will sadden her.
Guide The Mayor through the forest and defeat the wolves while attempting to
avoid killing other creatures (directly) and destroying other obstacles.
